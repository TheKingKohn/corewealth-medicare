Frequently Asked Questions

When should I enroll in Medicare?

Your Initial Enrollment Period begins 3 months before you turn 65 and lasts through 3 months after your birthday month. Missing this window can result in penalties, so it's important to plan ahead.

What's the difference between Medicare Advantage and Medicare Supplement?

Medicare Advantage (Part C) replaces Original Medicare with an all-in-one plan. Medicare Supplement (Medigap) works alongside Original Medicare to help cover out-of-pocket costs. I'll help you determine which is best for your situation.

Is there a cost for your services?

No! My consultations are completely free. I'm compensated by the insurance carriers, so there's no cost to you for my expertise and guidance.

Can I switch plans if I'm not happy?

Yes! During the Annual Enrollment Period (October 15 - December 7) and Medicare Advantage Open Enrollment (January 1 - March 31), you can make changes to your coverage. Some Special Enrollment Periods may also apply.
